WHAT WE DO

Electromagnetic Environment Sanitization

The Nothing Box creates a controlled electromagnetic environment using a sealed Faraday cage, precision frequency generators, and multi-sensor data collection. During a session, specific frequencies are introduced into the space while sensors measure your body's natural bioelectric response.

The process is called sanitization. The hypothesis is that by creating a quiet electromagnetic space, free from the ambient noise of WiFi, cellular signals, and electronic devices, the body's own electromagnetic signature becomes measurable and may shift in response to introduced frequencies.

This is a hypothesis, not a proven medical outcome. We do not claim that sanitization treats, cures, or prevents any medical condition. What we offer is an environment and a measurement. What your body does in that environment is between you and your body.

The Frequencies

Sessions use a combination of frequencies drawn from several traditions and areas of research:

SOLFEGGIO FREQUENCIES A set of tones (174 Hz through 963 Hz) with historical roots in sacred music. Some practitioners and researchers hypothesize that these frequencies interact with the body's bioelectric systems. Peer-reviewed research is limited but growing.

EARTH FREQUENCIES The Schumann Resonance (7.83 Hz) and related natural electromagnetic frequencies that exist in the space between the Earth's surface and ionosphere. These are well-documented in geophysics. Their relationship to human biology is an active area of study.

THE FARADAY CAGE A sealed metal enclosure that blocks external electromagnetic interference. This is established physics, not a hypothesis. What the Faraday cage does is remove noise. What happens in the quiet is what we measure.

What We Measure

During your session, sensors collect data across multiple channels: coil voltage, pickup coil response, ECG (bioelectric), E-field (electrostatic), and 3D magnetic field. This data is analyzed after your session and compiled into a personalized report.

The report shows how your body's electromagnetic signature responded to each frequency. Some frequencies may produce strong responses. Others may produce silence. Both are meaningful. The data belongs to you.

YOUR SESSION

What Happens

1

You lay down

On a massage table, fully clothed, eyes closed if you'd like. That's the hardest part.

2

Frequencies play

The Nothing Box introduces specific frequencies into the space. You may hear a faint hum. You may feel a subtle warmth or tingling. You may feel nothing at all. All normal.

3

Sensors measure

Small sensors near your wrists and around the box measure how your body's electromagnetic signature responds. This is passive measurement. Nothing enters your body.

4

You receive your report

After your session, the data is analyzed and compiled into a personalized report. The report is yours to keep.

45 minutes
Total session time including setup and breakdown
WHAT WE NEED FROM YOU

Before Your Session

  • A quiet room. The session requires minimal noise and interruption for the full 45 minutes.
  • If children are present in the home, please arrange for them to be in another room for the duration.
  • Enough space for a massage table. Roughly 6 feet by 3 feet of open floor space.
  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. No metal jewelry, watches, or belts during the session.
  • No heavy meals within 30 minutes. Light and hydrated is ideal.
  • Phone on silent and in another room if possible.
REMINDERS

Please remember:

No pacemakers, implanted defibrillators, insulin pumps, or implanted electronic medical devices.

This is a wellness experience, not a medical treatment. If anything feels wrong, say so immediately and we stop.

You can end the session at any time for any reason. No questions asked.
